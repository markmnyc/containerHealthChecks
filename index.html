About

I'm a Technical Team Lead at FNB South Africa with over a decade of experience building, migrating, and maturing enterprise banking systems. I currently lead a team of 16 engineers — my focus is on clear direction, growing the people around me, and shipping software that actually moves the business forward.

My biggest delivery as lead has been a fleet management platform that consolidated business capabilities across divisions, giving fleet companies a single place to manage their operations. Before moving into leadership, I spent years in the engine room — building and migrating core FNB systems that are now some of the bank's most relied-upon platforms.

Outside of FNB I'm exploring AI — particularly what LLMs and agents can do for enterprise software teams. This site doubles as my lab notebook for those experiments.
